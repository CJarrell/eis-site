Elite Packages

5 professionally curated style upgrades, for every type of modern lady

  • Discovery Package

    When you are ready to make improvements to your image but aren’t sure how to take the first step, The Discovery Package gives you a wonderful starting point.  Together, we’ll identify the clothing, styles and colors that allow you to shine.The Discovery Package begins with an initial assessment and thought provoking style exercises. From there, we’ll begin the Image Consultation to identify your physical characteristics, style preferences and the strategies to maximize all of your most flattering features.
    Next, we will get you wearing the right colors that flatter you the most. You’ll not only find out what those colors are during the Custom Color Analysis, but you’ll also receive a portable swatch book so you won’t forget.

  • Job Seeker Package

    It’s true what they say: you only have one chance to make a first impression, and in today’s competitive job market, why not make that first impression a confident one? The Job Seeker Package includes a Targeted Personal Shopping Session and Resume Review.

  • Special Event Package

    Whether you have a fundraiser, high school reunion or other special event planned, you know that you want to present your best self.  You only have to worry about enjoying yourself after being dressed impeccably for the special occasion.
    The Special Event Package includes a targeted Personal Shopping Session, Outfit Styling, make up style/hair suggestion and a boost of confidence!

  • Personal Stylist Package

    Have you already done a lot of thinking about your desired image, maybe even received an image consultation previously, but need more help getting started?  You are not alone. Image building can be overwhelming and full of expensive mistakes if done incorrectly.  As your stylist, I can help you quickly achieve the look you’ve been wanting.
    All of the services in The Personal Stylist Package are designed to help you build a wardrobe that is filled with key pieces that look great on you.  We’ll start with the information that you already have about your best styles, cuts and colors and take the necessary steps to get you looking fabulous. The Personal Stylist Package begins with taking a good look at what your closet has to offer (and what needs to go) during the Wardrobe Makeover.  We’ll purge the unflattering “distractions” and leave you with a closet that is filled with items that work for you.  Then we’ll spend time mixing and matching outfits in new ways so that you can hit the ground running.
    And if we find that you are missing essential wardrobe items, we’ll solve that during the Personal Shopping Session.  This is when you try on the ensembles that have been preselected for you based on your size, best colors, body type and budget.  All is geared to maximizing your wardrobe and minimizing your frustration.
    We’ll round out your image awareness with a Styling Lesson that not only empowers you with dressing tips and advice, but also gives more insight into accessorizing and adding polish to your new look.

  • Elite Package

    What does your image, or personal brand, say about you?  Actually, it is saying quite a lot… all of the time.  Just think about it.  When you are in the store, do you buy the item that’s in a dented box with no decoration or the one in a well-contructed, visually appealing container?
    Truth is that we get sized up by others based on how well we package ourselves.  I want them to be impressed when they look at you!  The Image Master Package examines your features, wardrobe and desired style to create the look that displays your personal brand confidently.
